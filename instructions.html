How the Recommendation System Works

The recommendation system employs an algorithm to analyze the characteristics of songs in your favorite playlist to determine your unique music preferences. You then choose a genre you're curious about exploring and based on your musical history and preferences, the recommendation system will curate three song recommendations tailored for you. If you are not a big fan of creating personal playlists, you can simply share your music taste by making your Spotify Wrapped public. This involves duplicating your Spotify Wrapped playlist in order to access a shareable link. Here's how you can do it:

Step 1

Go to Spotify Wrapped.

Step 2

Click on the three dots.

Step 3

Click on 'Add to other playlist'.

Step 4

Select 'New Playlist'.

Step 5

Copy the link using Share.
